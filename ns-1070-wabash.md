🚂 NS 1070 – Wabash

Welcome to the Wabash Heritage Exhibit.

Norfolk Southern Heritage Unit NS 1070 honors the legacy of the historic Wabash Railroad, a railroad recognized for its distinctive blue locomotives and its famous slogan, “Follow the Flag.” The Wabash served communities throughout the Midwest and played an important role in freight and passenger transportation before becoming part of the Norfolk & Western Railway.

This exhibit preserves my personal history with NS 1070, documenting every catch, photograph, video, and milestone as part of the TrainsForever Archive Museum. As additional encounters are documented, this exhibit will continue to grow and reflect my ongoing journey with the Wabash Heritage Unit.

📝 Curator’s Notes

NS 1070 – Wabash is one of Norfolk Southern’s original Heritage Units, honoring the historic Wabash Railroad. At the time of this exhibit’s publication, I have documented 4 catches, all of which have been trailing catches.

Although I have not yet documented NS 1070 leading a train, this exhibit preserves my encounters through photographs, videos, and personal records. It will continue to grow as new catches are documented and milestones are achieved as part of the TrainsForever Archive Museum.

Every documented catch helps preserve the history of this heritage unit for future railfans to enjoy.
